Coffee means a lot to us - but that's not all! We are also passionate about other hot and cold drinks, pastries, sandwiches and salads.

We make your favorite meal every day with knowledge, love and the best ingredients of the season.

Cold Drinks

With us you will find not only hot coffee, but also a large selection of cold drinks. In addition to cold drinks in bottles, we have our own mixes that we make while you wait. In the summer heat we can recommend you to try one of our iced teas, frapinos or iced tea! If you want a fresh and healthy alternative, we can tempt you with our smoothies, made with natural ingredients. Welcome in and find your favorite!

Hot drinks

We love coffee more than anything else. With us you will find several exciting flavor combinations based on our own Coffee House. We have more on the menu than just good coffee - we serve, for example, cocoa made with a combo of Belgian dark and milk chocolate. The big favorite chai latte never fails and you can choose between 5 different teas. You do not have to love coffee to find something good with us!
